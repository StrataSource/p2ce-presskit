About

Hello member of the press and/or interested P2CE fan! This is a collection of files you can use when reporting on P2CE.

Portal 2: Community Edition is a free community-maintained overhaul framework for Portal 2, designed to modernize and massively expand the game's modding capabilities.

Built on Strata Source (a heavily modified branch of Valve's Source Engine), it introduces modern rendering technology, expands engine limitations and adds a full scripting framework with AngelScript, giving creators full control over the game's content and behavior and enabling them to create anything from custom puzzles to full game campaigns.

For Portal 2 fans, Portal 2: Community Edition is a hub that lets them play Portal related content easier and faster than ever before.

The game is available in our open beta for free on Steam, and is in active development.
